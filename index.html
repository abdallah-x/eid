L'3id Mbarek ya Aya 🌙

F had l'3id lkbir, awel haja tjini f bali ntiya
Parce que ntiya lli katkhalli kol yawm ikoun 3id 🌹

Allah ya3tik saha, sa3ada, o kol ma katmni lrasek
Kol 3am o ntiya b'alf khir, ya ghla chi haja 3andi 💛

❤️ 🌙 ⭐ 🌙 ❤️
🥀

Risala mn l'qelb 💔

3aref belli ghalt, o 3aref belli klamي o tsarrofati wadjou'ek

Mangolich "smahli" bach nkammel l'mawdo3...
Kangoliha lأنها safi asafi mn qelbi 💔

Ntiya aghla haja 3endi f had ddenya
O akhir haja nbghiha dir hiya nwejja3 lmra lli kanbghiha

Kanبدا had l'3id b'l i3tiraf belli l'ghalta kanet mni
O kanwa3dek — ghadi nkoun l'chi lli katستاهلي 🤍

"Ana asif... o kanbghik bzzaf"

🌟

Ntiya kol haja 💛

Ntiya machi ghir sahbti o ḥbibti...
Ntiya dfa f nhar lberd, o daḥka f nhar zzenga 🌹

Ma3a wjoudi, ddenya katbqa aḥla b'ktir
O ana makanqderch ntasawwar ḥyati biladik 🌙

Allah ykhlik liya, ya ghla chi 3andi ❤️

3id Mbarek 🌙✨
